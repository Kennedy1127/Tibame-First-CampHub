Campcover

CAMPCOVER include things like theft or damage to your camping equipment, as well as liability protection in case someone is injured on your campsite.

CAMPCOVER also cover trip cancellations or interruptions due to unforeseen events, such as severe weather or illness. It's important to read the details of any camping insurance policy carefully to understand what is and isn't covered.

About Campground

The campground features tent camping sites that are nestled among trees and along the banks of the Upper Ammonoosuc River.

Upper Valley offers campers a peaceful and quiet atmosphere, as cars and RVs are not allowed in the camping area.
